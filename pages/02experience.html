Do Random Acts of Kindness (DoRAK): President Leadership

Being a part of DoRAK is one of my proudest contributions during university. As said by Leo Buscagalia, "Too often we underestimate the power of a touch, a smile, a kind word, a listening ear, an honest compliment, or the smallest act of caring, all of which have the potential to turn a life around." DoRAK's mission is to provide that gesture that can brighten one's day with the minimum time and resources required of the students in the organization. Takeaways: It is surprisingly hard to give away free things; people understand bake sales or donation seekers but are frequently wary of genuinely free things.
