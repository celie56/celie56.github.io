Thomson Reuters: Software Engineer C++ C# JavaScript

One of the products that my parents use in their small CPA firm is Thomson Reuters UltraTax. Working with Thomson Reuters was a joy as I knew that my work went directly back to the stakeholders in my life. My first summer interning with TR, I accomplished my task of developing a new sub-product to handle 990 returns for the state of Kansas. Given my performance that first summer I was invited to return on the higher team that handles the federal individual income tax return (1040 US). Here I developed a new portion of the product to handle the ABLE portion of new Affordable Care Act legislation as well as organized intern activities.

Do Random Acts of Kindness (DoRAK): President Leadership

Being a part of DoRAK is one of my proudest contributions during university. As said by Leo Buscagalia, "Too often we underestimate the power of a touch, a smile, a kind word, a listening ear, an honest compliment, or the smallest act of caring, all of which have the potential to turn a life around." DoRAK's mission is to provide that gesture that can brighten one's day with the minimum time and resources required of the students in the organization. Interesting side note: It is surprisingly hard to give away free things; people understand bake sales or donation seekers but are frequently wary of genuinely free things.
